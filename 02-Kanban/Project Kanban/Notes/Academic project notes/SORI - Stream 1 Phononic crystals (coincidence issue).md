**Kushwaha et al. (1993):** Kushwaha, M. S., Halevi, P., Dobrzynski, L., & Djafari-Rouhani, B. (1993). Acoustic band structure of periodic elastic composites. _Physical Review Letters, 71_(13), 2022–2025. [https://doi.org/10.1103/PhysRevLett.71.2022](https://doi.org/10.1103/PhysRevLett.71.2022)

What they are doing: 
- Aiming to block at eigenfrequencies all vibration transmission completely regardless of k value or polarization
- They demonstrate this is possible by mapping out sound transmission for a nickel on aluminum matrix and a aluminum on nickel matrix.
